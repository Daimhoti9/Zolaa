About Us

Enjoy Italian Cusine,quality and hearty preperation Pizza Zola is a quaint Italian restaurant on Warschauer Str. in the middle of Friedrichshain. From 10am until 1am Pizza Zola offers delicious freshly prepared food at great prices. From homemade pasta to pizza and salads to tarte flambée and antipasti, there is a diverse selection for both big and small appetites. Visit Pizza Zola - it's worth it!
